A-Share vs US Stock Rules

Understand the key differences between trading in China's A-share market and US stock markets.

Frequently asked questions

Why no US-style circuit breaker in China now?

China removed its index circuit breaker in 2016 after it backfired; it relies on per-stock price limits instead.

T+1 vs T+0?

China settles T+1 (no same-day selling); US allows same-day trading though settlement is T+2.

Is Chinese capital gains tax lower?

Individual gains on A-shares are currently exempt, whereas US investors owe capital gains tax.
