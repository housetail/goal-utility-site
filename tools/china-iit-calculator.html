Frequently asked questions

Who must pay Chinese IIT?

Anyone earning China-sourced income who meets the tax-resident or source rules; expats working in China are generally taxed on China-sourced income.

What is the RMB 5,000 threshold?

It is the standard monthly comprehensive deduction (RMB 60,000/year) applied before tax.

Do tax treaties help?

Yes, China has treaties that may reduce or exempt tax for temporary visitors; the 183-day and 6-year rules matter.

Is there a separate capital gains tax?

Individual gains from A-shares are currently exempt; other gains may be taxed.
