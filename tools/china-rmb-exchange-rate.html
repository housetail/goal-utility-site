China RMB Exchange Rate

Convert an amount and learn how the PBOC central parity rate (中间价) sets each day's reference for the onshore RMB.

Rates are indicative mid-market rates fetched live from a public exchange-rate API and may differ from the rate your bank or money changer applies (which includes a spread and fees). The PBOC central parity (中间价) is the daily reference rate published by the People's Bank of China; the onshore RMB (CNY) may trade within a band of roughly ±2% around it, while the offshore RMB (CNH) trades more freely and can differ slightly. Rates change constantly and this tool is for estimation only.

Frequently asked questions

What is the PBOC central parity?

The daily reference rate set by the People's Bank of China; the onshore RMB may move within a band around it.

What is the difference between CNY and CNH?

CNY is onshore RMB (regulated band); CNH is offshore RMB traded outside mainland China with a freer price.

Why does RMB have a daily band?

To limit intraday volatility; the band has widened over time to about ±2% around the parity.
