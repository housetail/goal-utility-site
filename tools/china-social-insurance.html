Frequently asked questions

What is 五险一金?

The "five insurances and one fund": pension, medical, unemployment, work injury, maternity insurance, plus the housing provident fund.

Who pays?

Both employee and employer contribute; the employee's share is withheld from salary.

Do rates vary by city?

Yes, contribution rates and the wage base caps differ across cities.
