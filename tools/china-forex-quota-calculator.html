Frequently asked questions

What is the $50,000 quota?

Each individual may purchase or remit up to USD 50,000 of foreign currency per year under simplified procedures.

Can I send more than $50,000?

Yes, with supporting documents proving a genuine purpose; the bank reviews and reports large transfers.

Does it roll over?

No, the quota resets each calendar year and unused amounts are forfeited.

Is this for RMB exchange or remittance?

Both purchasing foreign currency and outward remittance count toward the same annual quota.
