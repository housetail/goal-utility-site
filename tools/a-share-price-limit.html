Chinese mainland stocks have daily price limits. Enter the previous close to see today's limit-up (涨停) and limit-down (跌停) prices.

Frequently asked questions

What are 涨停 and 跌停?

Limit-up (涨停) and limit-down (跌停) are the maximum prices a stock may trade at on a given day, set as a percentage of the prior close.

What is T+1?

Trades settle the next day; shares bought today cannot be sold until tomorrow.

Which boards use 20%?

The STAR Market (科创板) and ChiNext (创业板) use a ±20% limit; the main board uses ±10%.

Are there exceptions?

Newly listed stocks and some restructured stocks may trade without a limit for their first days.
