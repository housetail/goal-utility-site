Frequently asked questions

What is LPR?

The Loan Prime Rate is the benchmark reference rate set by the National Interbank Funding Center in China; most mortgages are priced as LPR plus a fixed spread.

Equal installment vs equal principal?

Equal installment (等额本息) keeps the monthly payment constant; equal principal (等额本金) pays the same principal each month so payments start high and decrease, with less total interest.

Can foreigners get a mortgage in China?

Yes, but banks typically require a valid work permit/residence permit, proof of income, and a higher down payment; policies vary by city.

Are rates fixed?

Chinese mortgages usually use a floating rate reset periodically against LPR, not a long-term fixed rate like in the US.
