Frequently asked questions

What is 调休?

China shifts rest days around holidays so workers get long breaks; some weekends become make-up workdays and remain trading days.

When is the market closed?

Weekends plus the official public-holiday schedule published by the exchanges each year.

What are the trading hours?

Morning 9:30-11:30 and afternoon 13:00-15:00 (Beijing time), Monday to Friday.
