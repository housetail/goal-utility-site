Frequently asked questions

What is the Housing Provident Fund?

A mandatory savings scheme where employers and employees both contribute; members can borrow at a preferential rate for housing.

Who qualifies?

Contributors with a sufficient continuous payment history; the exact rules vary by city.

What is the interest rate?

Provident-fund loans carry a below-market rate (around 3.1% recently), which is lower than commercial LPR mortgages.
