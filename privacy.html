Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-07-18

Overview

China Finance Tools provides free, browser-based calculators that explain Chinese-specific financial rules to English-speaking users. This policy describes what data we collect and how we use it.

Information we collect

Our calculators run entirely in your browser. The numbers you enter (loan amounts, salaries, dates, etc.) are never sent to our servers — all calculations happen locally on your device.

Advertising

We use Google AdSense to display ads. AdSense may use cookies and unique identifiers to serve personalized ads and to measure performance. Google's use of advertising cookies is governed by the Google Ads Policy. You can manage ad personalization in your Google Ads Settings.

Analytics

We may use privacy-friendly, aggregated analytics to understand which tools are useful. This data is anonymized and not linked to individual identities.

Contact

If you have questions about this policy, contact us via the repository issues page on GitHub.
